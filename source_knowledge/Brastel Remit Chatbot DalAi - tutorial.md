\<Title\>HOW TO SIGN UP FOR BRASTEL REMIT APP (WITH A DRIVER'S LICENSE \- FOR JAPANESE NATIONALITY ONLY)\</Title\>  
\<Purpose\>In this tutorial, we will guide you through the simple and hassle-free process of signing up to the Brastel Remit app.  
Join us as we walk you through each step, ensuring a seamless sign-up experience. Our user-friendly interface and intuitive design make the registration process quick and enjoyable.\</Purpose\>  
\<Procedure\>  
1\. Open the **Brastel Remit** app and tap **CREATE AN ACCOUNT**  
2\. Agree to the Terms and Conditions by ticking the checkboxes and tap **NEXT**  
3\. Enter your email address twice and your password twice, and tap **Confirm**

**IMPORTANT: STORE YOUR PASSWORD**  
4\. Enter the “verification code” you received by email  
5\. In the “Your email has been verified“ screen, tap **NEXT**  
6\. Select **Japanese** as your nationality  
7\. Select **Driver's License**  
8\. Read the information and tap **NEXT**  
9\. Read the information and tap **NEXT**  
10\. Read the instructions and tap **TAKE PHOTO** to take a photo of the front side of your driver's license  
10.1. Align the ID within the frame  
11\. Confirm that the photo is not blurred, nor with flash glare or cut off, and tap **NEXT.** To retake the photo, tap **CHANGE PHOTO**  
12\. Read the instructions and tap **TAKE PHOTO** to take a photo of your driver's license from an angle  
12.1. Tilt your device 45 degrees and align the ID within the frame  
13\. Confirm that the photo is not blurred, nor with flash glare or cut off, and that shows its thickness and tap **NEXT**. To retake the photo, tap **CHANGE PHOTO**  
14\. Read the instructions and tap **TAKE PHOTO** to take a photo of the back side of your driver's license  
14.1. Align the back of the ID within the frame  
15\. Confirm that the photo is not blurred, nor with flash glare or cut off, and tap **NEXT.** To retake the photo, tap **CHANGE PHOTO**  
16\. Tap **TAKE PHOTO** to take a photo of your face  
17\. Confirm that the photo is not blurred, nor cut off, and tap **NEXT.** To retake the photo, tap **CHANGE PHOTO**  
18\. Select your occupation and enter your telephone number. Tap **NEXT.** If you have a promotion code, enter it and then tap **NEXT**  
19. Setup a 4-digit PIN Number. Tap **NEXT**

**IMPORTANT: STORE YOUR PIN**  
20. Enter your name in Roman letters. Review the information and tick the acknowledgement checkbox. Tap **SUBMIT REGISTRATION**  
21. Done\! You will receive a message on your app once your account registration has been approved (in approximately 1 to 3 business days)  
**IMPORTANT:** If your account registration has not been approved within the given timeframe, check your notifications on the bell icon located on the top right corner of the app, and follow the instructions provided.\</Procedure\>

\</Tutorial\>
