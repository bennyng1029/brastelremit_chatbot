**Question:** Is there some fee charged to the recipient when receiving the money in his/her country?  
**Answer:** This depends on the country. For example, for Brazil there is no extra fee charged to receive the money. For other countries please check with our customer center.

**Question:** How long does it take for the money to arrive in Brazil?   
**Answer:** Generally, the transfers will be paid via PIX within a few hours, provided all the information is correct.
