# Deposit Guide - Other Banks (No Account)

## Purpose
This guide explains how to deposit funds at various Japanese banks (SMBC, Mitsubishi UFJ, Resona, etc.) even if you do not have an account with them.

## Supported Banks
- **SMBC** (Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation)
- **MUFG** (Mitsubishi UFJ Bank)
- **Resona Bank**
- **Other Local Banks**

## General Steps
1. Open the **Brastel Remit** app.
2. Tap **Menu** -> **How to add money** -> **No Bank Account**.
3. Select your desired bank from the list.
4. Follow the specific ATM or counter instructions displayed in the app for that bank.
5. **IMPORTANT: You MUST enter your personal DEPOSIT CODE during the process as instructed by the app.**
