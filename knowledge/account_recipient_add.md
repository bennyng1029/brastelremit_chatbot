# Adding a Recipient Guide

## Purpose
This guide explains how to add various types of recipients (Agent Collect Point, Bank Account, E-Wallet, bKash) in the Brastel Remit app.

---

## Common Initial Steps
1. Open the **Brastel Remit** app.
2. Tap **Menu** (3 horizontal bars located at the top left).
3. Tap **Recipients**.
4. Tap **Add Recipient**.
5. Enter your **PIN**.
6. Tap **ENTER RECIPIENT INFORMATION**.
7. Tap **Destination** and select the destination country.

---

## Option 1: Agent Collect Point (Cash Pickup)
1. Select **Cash Pickup / Agent** as Delivery Method.
2. Choose the specific **Agent Name** from the list.
3. Tap **NEXT**.
4. Enter recipient’s full name, nationality, address, city/state, telephone number, relationship, reason for transfer, and fund source.
5. Tap **NEXT**.
6. Review the information and tap **SAVE**.

---

## Option 2: Bank Account Deposit
1. Select **Bank account deposit** as Delivery Method.
2. Tap **Bank Name** and enter/select the name of the bank from the list.
3. Read the "Verify Account Number" pop-up.
4. Enter the **Account Number** and select the **Bank Account Type**.
   * *Note: Some destinations may require Swift code or Routing number.*
5. Tap **NEXT**.
6. Enter recipient’s details (Name, Nationality, Address, etc.).
7. Tap **NEXT**.
8. Review the information and tap **SAVE**.

---

## Option 3: E-Wallet / Mobile Wallet
1. Select **Mobile wallet / E-Wallet** as Delivery Method.
2. Select the **Agent Name** from the list and enter the **Account Number**.
3. Tap **NEXT**.
4. Enter recipient’s details (Name, Nationality, Address, etc.).
5. Tap **NEXT**.
6. Review the information and tap **SAVE**.

---

## Option 4: bKash (Bangladesh)
1. Select **bKash Account** as Delivery Method.
2. Enter the **bKash Account Number**.
3. Tap **NEXT**.
4. Enter recipient’s details (Name, Nationality, Address, etc.).
5. Tap **NEXT**.
6. Review the information and tap **SAVE**.
