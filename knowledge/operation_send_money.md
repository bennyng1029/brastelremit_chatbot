# Sending Money Guide

## Purpose
This guide walks you through the steps to execute a money transfer using the Brastel Remit app once your account is approved and you have added a recipient.

## Steps
1. Open the **Brastel Remit** app.
2. Tap **Menu** (3 horizontal bars located at the top left).
3. Tap **Home**.
4. Scroll through your list of recipients and select the one who will receive the money.
5. Tap **send money**.
6. In the **MY BALANCE** screen:
   * Enter the amount to send in **Yen** OR the receiving amount in the **local currency**.
   * Confirm the exchange rate and amounts.
   * Verify the recipient name and delivery details (Bank/Agent).
   * Select the **Fund Source** and **Reason for Transfer**.
7. Tap **NEXT**.
8. Review the final transfer summary and tap **NEXT**.
9. Agree to the terms and conditions by ticking the checkbox.
10. Tap **Confirm**.

## Completion
Your order is now being processed. You can check the status of your transfer in the app's history or notifications.
