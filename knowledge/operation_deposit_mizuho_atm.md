# Deposit Guide - Mizuho Bank ATM (No Account)

## Purpose
This guide explains how to deposit funds into your Brastel Remit account using a Mizuho Bank ATM, even if you do not have a Mizuho bank account.

## Steps
1. Open the **Brastel Remit** app.
2. Tap **Menu** (3 horizontal bars located at the top left).
3. Tap **How to add money**.
4. Tap **No Bank Account**.
5. Tap **Mizuho**.
6. At the Mizuho ATM, follow the step-by-step instructions displayed in the app.
   * **IMPORTANT: Do not forget to enter your personal DEPOSIT CODE (shown on step 14 in the app guide).**
