# Identification Update & Re-upload Guide

## Purpose
This guide explains how to update your identification document or re-submit photos if your initial registration was not approved.

## When to Update your ID
- If your ID has expired.
- If your account was not approved due to blurred or unclear document photos.
- If you have changed your address or other personal information.

## Steps to Update in the App
1. Open the **Brastel Remit** app.
2. Tap **Menu** (3 horizontal bars located at the top left).
3. Tap **My Account**.
4. Tap **Profile**.
5. Scroll down and tap **Update Document**.
6. Follow the prompts to take new photos:
   * **Front side**: Tap "Take a picture of the front side".
   * **Back side**: Tap "Take a picture of the back side".
7. Enter your **Identification document number**.
8. Enter the **Expiration date**.
9. If your address has changed, enter the new address details.
10. Tap **NEXT**.
11. Review the information. Tap **SAVE** if correct, or **EDIT** to make changes.

## Alternative Update Method (Email)
If you are unable to use the app to update your ID, you can send clear photos of the front and back of your identification document to:
- **Email**: id_update@brastelremit.jp
- **Note**: Please include your registered name and telephone number in the email.

## Troubleshooting Rejected Documents
If your registration was not approved:
- Check the **notification bell icon** (top right corner of the app) for specific feedback.
- Ensure photos are not blurred, have no flash glare, and no parts are cut off.
- For Resident Cards and My Number Cards, ensuring the **thickness** of the card is visible in the angled photo is critical.
