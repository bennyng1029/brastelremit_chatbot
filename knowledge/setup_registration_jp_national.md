# Registration Guide - Japanese Nationals (Driver's License)

## Purpose
This tutorial guides Japanese nationality customers through the process of signing up for the Brastel Remit app using a Driver's License.

## Steps
1. Open the **Brastel Remit** app and tap **CREATE AN ACCOUNT**.
2. Agree to the Terms and Conditions and tap **NEXT**.
3. Enter your email address twice and your password twice, then tap **Confirm**.
   * **IMPORTANT: STORE YOUR PASSWORD SECURELY.**
4. Enter the verification code received by email.
5. On the “Your email has been verified” screen, tap **NEXT**.
6. Select **Japanese** as your nationality.
7. Select **Driver's License** as your document type.
8. Follow the on-screen instructions to take photos of your document:
   * **Front side**: Align the ID within the frame and tap **TAKE PHOTO**.
   * **At an angle**: Tilt your device 45 degrees to show the ID's thickness.
   * **Back side**: Align the back of the ID and take a photo.
9. Tap **TAKE PHOTO** to take a photo of your face.
10. Confirm all photos are clear (no blur or glare) and tap **NEXT**.
11. Select your occupation and enter your telephone number. Tap **NEXT**.
12. If you have a promotion code, enter it; otherwise, tap **NEXT**.
13. Set up a 4-digit PIN Number.
    * **IMPORTANT: STORE YOUR PIN SECURELY.**
14. Enter your name in Roman letters, review the information, and tap **SUBMIT REGISTRATION**.

## Approval
Verification takes approximately **1 to 3 business days**. You will receive a notification in the app once approved. 
*Note: If not approved within this timeframe, check the notification bell icon at the top right for instructions.*
