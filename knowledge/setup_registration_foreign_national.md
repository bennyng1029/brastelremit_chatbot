# Registration Guide - Foreign Nationals (Residence Card / Zairyu Card)

## Purpose
This tutorial guides foreign nationals through the process of signing up for the Brastel Remit app using a Residence Card (Zairyu Card), covering both NFC-enabled devices and devices without NFC.

---

## Method 1: Using NFC-Enabled Device
### Steps
1. Open the **Brastel Remit** app and tap **CREATE AN ACCOUNT**.
2. Agree to the Terms and Conditions and tap **NEXT**.
3. Enter your email and password, then tap **Confirm**.
   * **IMPORTANT: STORE YOUR PASSWORD SECURELY.**
4. Enter the verification code received by email and tap **NEXT**.
5. Select **Non-Japanese** as your nationality.
6. Enter your **Zairyu Card number** and tap **NEXT**.
7. Tap **START** to scan the NFC chip of your card.
   * **Tip**: Hold the ID against the NFC reader of the device (usually on the back) until successfully read. Hold it in your palm rather than on a table.
8. Follow instructions to take a photo of the **back side** of your Zairyu Card.
9. Tap **TAKE PHOTO** for a face photo (captured automatically when in focus).
10. Select your occupation and enter your telephone number.
11. Set up a 4-digit PIN Number.
    * **IMPORTANT: STORE YOUR PIN SECURELY.**
12. Review the info and tap **SUBMIT REGISTRATION**.

## Method 2: Device Without NFC
### Steps
1. Follow the same initial steps (1-5) as Method 1.
2. Select **Non-Japanese** as your nationality.
3. Take photos of your Zairyu Card:
   * **Front side**: Align within the frame and take the photo.
   * **At an angle**: Tilt your device 45 degrees to show the card's thickness.
   * **Back side**: Align and take the photo.
4. Tap **TAKE PHOTO** for a face photo (captured automatically when in focus).
5. Complete the occupation, phone number, and PIN setup.
6. Review and tap **SUBMIT REGISTRATION**.

## Approval
Verification takes approximately **1 to 3 business days**. You will receive a notification in the app once approved. 
*Note: If not approved within this timeframe, check the notification bell icon at the top right for instructions.*
