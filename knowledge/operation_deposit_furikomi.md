# Deposit Guide - Bank Transfer (Furikomi)

## Purpose
This guide explains how to make a standard bank transfer (Furikomi) from your personal Japanese bank account to Brastel Remit's dedicated accounts.

## Steps
1. Open the **Brastel Remit** app.
2. Tap **Menu** (3 horizontal bars located at the top left).
3. Tap **How to add money**.
4. Tap **Bank Transfer**.
5. Select **Other Banks** (or your specific bank if listed, like JP Bank).
6. Read the provided bank account details (Bank name, Branch, Account type, Account number).
7. **IMPORTANT: You MUST include your personal DEPOSIT CODE in the "Remitter Name" field when making the transfer. Failure to do so will delay your deposit.**
8. Proceed with the transfer via your bank's ATM or Internet Banking app.
