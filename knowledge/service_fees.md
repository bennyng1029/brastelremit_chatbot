# Brastel Remit Transfer Fees

## Transfer Fee Schedule (Japan to Overseas)
Fees are determined by the total transfer amount in JPY:

| Transfer Amount (JPY) | Transfer Fee (JPY) |
| :--- | :--- |
| 1 - 10,000 | **480 JPY** |
| 10,001 - 30,000 | **780 JPY** |
| 30,001 - 100,000 | **1,280 JPY** |
| 100,001 - 800,000 | **1,480 JPY** |

## Important Notes
- **Maximum Limit**: The maximum limit per transaction is **800,000 JPY**.
- **Daily Limit**: The maximum daily transfer limit is **1,000,000 JPY**.
- **Recipient Fees**: Some countries (like Brazil) have no additional fees for the recipient. For others, please check the destination specific FAQ.
- **Rates**: Exchange rates are updated frequently and can be checked in real-time within the app or via the [Online Simulator](https://brastelremit.jp/eng/simulator).
