# Deposit Guide - Lawson Bank ATM

## Purpose
This guide explains how to deposit funds into your Brastel Remit account using a Lawson Bank ATM.

## Steps
1. Visit any **Lawson Bank ATM**.
2. Insert your **Brastel Remit Card** (or Yucho Deposit Card if supported).
3. Follow the ATM prompts to deposit cash (JPY).
4. The funds will be credited to your Brastel Remit balance.

*Note: For detailed visual steps, please refer to the "How to add money" section in the Brastel Remit app under "Lawson Bank ATM".*
