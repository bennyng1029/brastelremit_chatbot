# Legal & Compliance: AML/CFT and ID Requirements

## 1. AML/CFT Policy
Brastel Co., Ltd. is committed to preventing money laundering and the financing of terrorism. Our framework includes:
- **Risk-Based Approach**: Continuous assessment of transaction risks.
- **Customer Due Diligence (KYC)**: Verification of identity and purpose of transaction.
- **Transaction Monitoring**: Automated systems to detect suspicious patterns.
- **Reporting**: Immediate reporting of suspicious transactions to the Financial Services Agency (FSA).

## 2. Identification Requirements
To comply with the "Act on Prevention of Transfer of Criminal Proceeds," we require:

### For Non-Japanese Nationals
- **Resident Card (Zairyu Card)**: Must be valid and show current address.

### For Japanese Nationals
- **Driver's License**
- **My Number Card**
- **Health Insurance Card** (Requires secondary document)

### My Number (Individual Number)
All customers, regardless of nationality, must provide their **My Number** documentation as per the "Act on the Use of Numbers to Identify a Specific Individual in Administrative Procedures."

## 3. ID Updates
If your card expires or you move, you must update your details:
- **Via App**: Upload new photos directly.
- **Via Email**: Send clear photos to `id_update@brastelremit.jp`.
