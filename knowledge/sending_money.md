# Sending Money with Brastel Remit

## Remittance Methods
1. **Mobile App**: Place requests 24/7. This is the primary method for managing recipients and tracking transfers.
2. **Remittance Card**: Use a personalized card at partner ATMs to send money directly to a pre-registered recipient without using the app.
3. **Counter**: Visit a physical Brastel location for assisted remittance.

## Transfer Limits
- **Minimum**: 1,000 JPY per transaction.
- **Maximum per Transaction**: 800,000 JPY.
- **Maximum per Day**: 1,000,000 JPY.
*Note: Limits may be subject to specific country regulations.*

## Delivery Times
- **Mobile Wallets (e.g., GCash, PayMaya)**: Immediate delivery.
- **Cash Pick-up (Agent)**: 15 minutes to 3 hours.
- **Bank Account**: 1 to 3 business days.
