# Deposit Methods and Locations

## JP Bank (Japan Post Bank)
- **ATM Deposits**: Use your **Yucho Deposit Card** at any JP Bank ATM across Japan.
- **Online**: Deposits via Yucho Direct internet banking.

## Convenience Stores & Partner ATMs
Using the Yucho Deposit Card, cash deposits can be made at:
- **Lawson Bank**: Available at Lawson convenience stores nationwide.
- **FamilyMart**: Available at E-net ATMs.
- **Daily Yamazaki / Ministop**: At participating ATM networks.

## Card Specifics
- **Yucho Deposit Card**: Exclusively for cash deposits into your Brastel Remit account.
- **Brastel Remit Card**: Used for triggering direct transfers at ATMs to specific recipients.
