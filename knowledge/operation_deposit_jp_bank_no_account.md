# Deposit Guide - Japan Post Bank (No Account)

## Purpose
This guide explains how to deposit funds at a Japan Post Bank branch if you do not have a Japanese bank account.

## Steps
1. Open the **Brastel Remit** app.
2. Tap **Menu** (3 horizontal bars located at the top left).
3. Tap **How to add money**.
4. Tap **No Bank Account**.
5. Tap **ゆうちょ銀行** (Japan Post Bank).
6. Fill out the **blue form** provided at the bank counter following the in-app instructions.
   * **IMPORTANT: You MUST enter your personal DEPOSIT CODE (indicated as number 7 on the form). This code is unique to your account.**
