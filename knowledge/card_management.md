# Card Management: Deposit vs. Remittance Cards

## 1. Yucho Deposit Card
- **Purpose**: To add money (JPY) to your Brastel Remit account balance.
- **Usage**: Use at any Japan Post Bank (Yucho) ATM.
- **Next Step**: After depositing, you must use the app to request a specific remittance.
- **Deposit Fees**:
    - Up to 100,000 JPY: **200 JPY**
    - Over 100,000 JPY: **400 JPY**

## 2. Brastel Remit Card (Linked)
- **Purpose**: For "One-Step" remittance.
- **Usage**: Each card is linked to **one specific beneficiary**.
- **Action**: Depositing money at an ATM using this card triggers an **automatic** transfer to that person.
- **Convenience**: No need to use the app after the ATM visit.

## 3. General Card Fees
- **Amendment/Inquiry**: 500 JPY
- **Refund Fee**: 750 JPY
- **Replacement**: If lost, contact support immediately to lock the card.

## 4. Security
- Never share your card PIN.
- Brastel will never ask for your card details via SMS or unofficial links.
