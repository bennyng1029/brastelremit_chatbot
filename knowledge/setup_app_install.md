# App Installation Guide

## iOS (iPhone)
### Purpose
Learn how to download the Brastel Remit app on your iOS device. We'll guide you through the steps to download and install our app in no time.

### Procedure
1. Open the **App Store** from the Home screen.
2. Tap **Search (検索)**.
3. Enter **Brastel Remit** and tap **Search**.
4. Tap **GET (入手)** and then **INSTALL** (If prompted, sign in to the App Store to complete the install).

*Note: If you have a compatible iPhone with Face ID enabled, double-click the Side button, then look at the screen to authenticate.*

## Android
### Purpose
Learn how to download the Brastel Remit app on your Android device. We'll guide you through the steps to download and install our app in no time.

### Procedure
1. Open the **Play Store** app.
2. Tap **Apps**.
3. Enter **Brastel Remit** and tap the search icon.
4. Tap **Install**.
