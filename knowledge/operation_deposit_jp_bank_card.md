# Deposit Guide - Japan Post Bank (with Deposit Card)

## Purpose
This tutorial explains how to deposit JPY into your Brastel Remit account using the Brastel Yucho Deposit Card at a Japan Post Bank (JP Bank) ATM.

## Steps
1. Open the **Brastel Remit** app.
2. Tap **Menu** (3 horizontal bars located at the top left).
3. Tap **How to add money**.
4. Tap **Brastel Yucho Card**.
5. Select **JAPAN POST ATM** (JP Bank).
6. At the ATM, insert your Brastel Yucho Deposit Card.
7. Follow the on-screen ATM instructions to complete the deposit.
