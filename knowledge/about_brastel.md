# About Brastel Co., Ltd.

## Corporate Profile
Brastel is a leading provider of international telecommunications and financial services in Japan.

- **License**: Type II Fund Transfer Service Provider (Director General of Kanto Financial Bureau No. 00016).
- **Headquarters**: 130-0015 Tokyo-to, Sumida-ku, Yokoami 2-6-2.
- **Founded**: Originally established as a telecom company, Brastel became famous for its rechargeable "Brastel Card" used for international calls.

## Our Mission
To bridge the gap between Japan and the rest of the world by providing affordable, transparent, and reliable financial services to the international community.

## Locations
While most services are handled online via the app, Brastel maintains a physical presence:
- **Main Office**: Sumida-ku, Tokyo (Near Ryogoku).
- **Partner Events**: We frequently host registration booths at community festivals (Filipino, Vietnamese, etc.) across Japan.
