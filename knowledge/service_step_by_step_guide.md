# Brastel Remit: 3-Step Quick Start Guide

## Get started and send money abroad on the same day!

### Step 1: Set Up Your Account
Download the app and sign up. You will need to upload your identification documents (Resident Card, My Number Card, or Driver's License) for verification. Once approved, you can add your recipient's details.

### Step 2: Deposit Funds
Deposit the amount you wish to send into your Brastel account. You can do this at any Japan Post Bank ATM, Lawson Bank ATM, or via a standard bank transfer (Furikomi).

### Step 3: Send Money
Select your recipient from the list, enter the sending amount, review the details, and confirm. Your transfer is now on its way!
