# Brastel Remit Registration Guide

## Overview
To start using Brastel Remit, users must register an account through the official mobile application. This process ensures compliance with Japanese financial regulations and anti-money laundering (AML) standards.

## Registration Steps
1. **App Installation**: Download the "Brastel Remit" app from the App Store or Google Play.
2. **Account Creation**: Register using a valid email address and verify it via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
3. **Document Submission**: Upload clear photographs of the following:
   - **Resident Card**: Both front and back sides are required.
   - **My Number Document**: Either a My Number Card (front/back) or a My Number Notification Card.
4. **Identity Verification**: The verification process typically takes up to **3 business days**. Users will receive an email once their account is activated.

## Alternative Registration
- **In-Person**: Registration is also available at Brastel Remit counters and special community events held across Japan.
